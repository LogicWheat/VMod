# Toolgun Controls
* Press "TAB" to open toolgun GUI
* After choosing a mode text hints in upper left will usually tell you what actions you can do with it
* Press "R" to reset mode to the beginning
* Press "Z" to delete created constraints/ventities
* You can also use "Strip Mode" to delete constraints/ventities
* You can create setting presets for certain modes. To change preset hold "LAlt" and scroll mouse wheel.
